About Me

Hey there! I’m a Design Computing graduate from the University of Sydney, art lover, tech enthusiast and aspiring UX designer who’s constantly looking for my next source of inspiration. I've always been driven to understand other people's experiences, and that includes getting to know the way they think. User experience research has let me learn and do just that, while still keeping up with my passion for all things art, tech and visual design.

I really value learning from new experiences, so I’m always open to any opportunities that come my way. If you feel the same, please don't hesitate to get in touch!
